FORE!FEIT

Excite Your Golf Game

The Concept

How It Works

FORE!FEIT is a card game played during your group's round of golf. Each card is tied to a common golfing event — when your golf shot triggers one of these events, you bare that card for each subsequent hole.

The only way to get rid of a card is for someone else to trigger the same event. The game rewards good play, punishes bad luck, and keeps every hole interesting.

The rules are intentionally open-ended as every group plays differently. Use the guidelines as a starting point and make the game your own.

Execution

GAMEPLAY FLOW

Once your game configuration deck is locked down, follow this standard cycle sequence over each progressive hole during your active group round.

01

The Trigger Event

As you play your natural golf layout, any player whose active swing sequence triggers a chosen deck card event (like hooking a ball directly into the water hazard) instantly "claims" ownership of that card.

02

Bearing the Burden

That player must host and track that card continuously across all incoming course holes. While holding a Consequence card, you must abide by its associated penalty constraints directly.

03

Passing the Token

The card remains locked to you until another competitor triggers that exact same event on a subsequent shot. Ownership then immediately shifts to them, clearing your plate.

04

Final Accounting

Upon finishing the final 18th cup green, any player stuck holding unresolved negative consequence metrics pays up the ultimate aggregate penalties to the group!
