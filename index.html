How to Play

Simple Rules.
Serious Bragging Rights.

FORE!FEIT drops into any round with any group. Pick your cards, play your game, and let the chaos unfold.

01

Pick Your Cards

Choose cards that suit your course — not every course has a bunker. Match the number of cards to the number of players.

02

Earn a Card

A card is awarded when its trigger event happens during a hole — ball in water, longest drive, chip-in, and more.

03

Carry It Forward

The card takes effect on the next hole. You hold it until someone else earns it — so play well, or pay the price.

04

Make It Yours

Mix consequences and rewards in the same game. Swap in custom forfeits. There are baseline rules, but how you play is up to your group.

The Cards

Know Your Cards.

Six consequence cards, six reward cards. Each comes with a default effect — but the library on this page gives you options to swap in your own.

⛱️bunker-buster.png

Trigger: Ball lands in a bunker

Bunker Buster

Your sand trap becomes your identity. Carry this card and everyone knows you found the beach.

🎯 Default: Only one club for the entire hole.
🚧fence-hopper.png

Trigger: Ball goes out of bounds

Fence Hopper

Your ball went for a walk. Now it's going to cost you.

🎯 Default: +1 stroke added to your hole score.
💧splash-zone.png

Trigger: Ball lands in water

Splash Zone

You found the water. Now the group gets to find your ball for you.

🎯 Default: Group throws your ball at any point during the hole.
🌿bush-whacker.png

Trigger: Ball lands in rough or bush

Bush Whacker

You went exploring. Now play with what you've got — just not the putter.

🎯 Default: Anything but a putter for the entire hole.
🌳tree-hugger.png

Trigger: Ball hits a tree

Tree Hugger

The fairway was right there. Instead, you hugged a tree.

🎯 Default: Blind tee shot — no looking before you swing.
🕵️the-houdini.png

Trigger: Ball is lost

The Houdini

Your ball vanished. Now your best shot will too.

🎯 Default: Reverse mulligan — the group picks your worst shot to replay.
🏌️par-fection.png

Trigger: Makes par

Par Fection

Solid, consistent, reliable. Par is the foundation — now build on it.

🎯 Default: Fix lie — improve your ball position once during the hole.
🥂chip-n-dip.png

Trigger: Chips in from off the green

Chip 'n Dip

You skipped the green and went straight to the bottom of the cup. Celebrate accordingly.

🎯 Default: Gimme Gimme — putts within a club length count as holed.
🦅eager-eagle.png

Trigger: Makes eagle

Eager Eagle

Two under. You earned the best reward in the bag.

🎯 Default: Relief anywhere — drop your ball wherever you like, no penalty.
⏱️early-bird.png

Trigger: First to finish a hole

Early Bird

Speed and efficiency rewarded. First one in earns the advantage.

🎯 Default: Play from front tees on the next hole.
📍pin-seeker.png

Trigger: Ball lands within a club length of the pin

Pin Seeker

Dead on target. That's the kind of shot that wins rounds.

🎯 Default: -1 stroke deducted from your hole score.
💨go-long.png

Trigger: Longest drive on the hole

Go Long

Big hitter, big reward. The longest ball off the tee earns the edge next hole.

🎯 Default: Start from best tee off — use the group's best tee shot position.
